My Experiences

Lexus

Apprentice Mechanic

I got the opportunity to work at a Lexus dealership as an apprentice mechanic in their service shop. During my 5 months at Lexus, I had the chance to familiarize myself with many pneumatic and power tools. I also had the opportunity to learn a bit about accessibility in design. Many of the parts in a vehicle are placed in quite difficult areas to access, and this has been able to open my eye in some of the flaws in designing a car. I aim to take my knowledge and design a car that is easier to repair than the current designs.
