More about me

I am Brandon Choi from Richmond Hill, Ontario. I am passionate about becoming an engineer to one day be able to design a car. I am interested in motor racing, particularly Formula 1, and hope to work for a team in the future. I have skills in design projects, woodworking, CAD designing, CNC machining, and 3D printing, which complements my engineering pursuits. I also have experience as a mechanic apprentice at Lexus. Currently, I am an incoming student at the University of Waterloo, where I will be studying mechanical engineering.

Experiences

Lexus

Apprentice Mechanic

I got the opportunity to work at a Lexus dealership as an apprentice mechanic in their service shop. During my 5 months at Lexus, I had the chance to familiarize myself with many pneumatic and power tools. I also had the opportunity to learn a bit about accessibility in design. Many of the parts in a vehicle are placed in quite difficult areas to access, and this has opened my eyes to some of the flaws in designing a car. I aim to take my knowledge and design a car that is easier to repair than the current designs.
